# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## How these personas are used in the exercise
- Use these persona definitions to frame scenarios and sample interactions in the Skills Exercise.
- Each persona can be used as a persona prompt for Copilot Spaces to shape role-specific guidance.

## Additional Suggested Personas (proposed additions)

The following personas are suggested to increase clarity, accountability, and smoother handoffs across project phases. Each persona includes a short role summary and a concrete set of responsibilities.

### Project Sponsor

**Role Summary**
The Project Sponsor is the senior executive who champions the project, secures funding, and makes high-level decisions.

**Responsibilities**
- Approve project budget, scope and major changes
- Provide strategic direction and remove organizational blockers
- Make or escalate decisions that exceed the project's delegated authority
- Advocate for the project with senior stakeholders

### Delivery Manager

**Role Summary**
The Delivery Manager focuses on optimizing flow and removing impediments so teams can deliver reliably.

**Responsibilities**
- Monitor delivery metrics (throughput, cycle time) and coach teams to improve
- Coordinate cross-team dependencies and release windows
- Facilitate escalation of risks and impediments to the Sponsor or leadership
- Run cadences that improve predictability (e.g., PI planning, release planning)

### Engineering Lead / Tech Lead

**Role Summary**
The Engineering Lead provides technical direction, architecture guidance and ensures engineering quality.

**Responsibilities**
- Define and validate architectural decisions and technical approaches
- Ensure engineering practices (code review, CI, testing) are followed
- Mentor engineers and unblock technical risks
- Balance short-term delivery with long-term maintainability

### Release Manager

**Role Summary**
The Release Manager owns release coordination, cutover plans and rollback strategies.

**Responsibilities**
- Create and own release checklists and runbooks
- Coordinate deployment windows across teams and environments
- Verify readiness criteria and sign-off before production releases
- Maintain rollback and incident playbooks for releases

### QA / Test Lead

**Role Summary**
The QA Lead ensures product quality by owning test strategy, automation and acceptance criteria.

**Responsibilities**
- Define test strategy and acceptance criteria for features and releases
- Coordinate automated and manual testing efforts
- Track quality metrics and prevent regressions
- Collaborate with engineering to improve testability

### DevOps / Platform Engineer

**Role Summary**
The DevOps/Platform Engineer owns CI/CD, hosting, observability and platform reliability.

**Responsibilities**
- Maintain CI/CD pipelines, infrastructure-as-code and deployment automation
- Ensure observability, monitoring and alerting are in place
- Collaborate on performance, scalability and incident response
- Support secure configuration and environment consistency

### Security & Compliance Officer

**Role Summary**
This persona ensures that projects meet security, privacy and regulatory requirements.

**Responsibilities**
- Review designs and releases for security and compliance risks
- Define controls and acceptance criteria for sensitive data handling
- Coordinate security assessments and remediation plans
- Maintain compliance checklists for audit readiness

### Business Analyst

**Role Summary**
The Business Analyst helps translate stakeholder needs into clear requirements and acceptance criteria.

**Responsibilities**
- Elicit and document requirements and business rules
- Create clear user stories with acceptance criteria
- Validate solutions with stakeholders and provide clarifications
- Support acceptance testing and sign-off

### UX Designer / Researcher

**Role Summary**
The UX Designer focuses on usability, accessibility and validating solutions with users.

**Responsibilities**
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Produce wireframes, prototypes and design specs
- Ensure accessibility and usability standards are met
- Validate UX acceptance criteria during verification

### Support / Operations Liaison

**Role Summary**
This persona represents support and operations teams to improve operational readiness and post-release support.

**Responsibilities**
- Provide input on runbooks and support processes
- Ensure support teams are trained on new features and known issues
- Coordinate on-call and escalation pathways
- Capture feedback and incidents for continuous improvement

### Stakeholder Representative

**Role Summary**
A designated stakeholder contact who provides domain expertise and business decisions during the project.

**Responsibilities**
- Represent stakeholder interests and priorities
- Provide timely decisions and clarifications
- Participate in key reviews and acceptance meetings
- Help validate that delivered outcomes meet business needs

---

These additional personas are optional and can be added to project documentation to clarify handoffs, approvals, and areas of ownership. For smaller projects several personas can be combined; for larger initiatives assigning distinct individuals to these roles improves accountability and traceability.
